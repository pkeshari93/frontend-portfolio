Mobile Hydrology was built in collaboration with scientists at Northern Arizona University and ACL Risk Estimator was built in collaboration with Erich Petushek (PhD, CSCS Assistant Professor College of Human Medicine - Marquette Campus)

Mobile hydrology is a web application where hydrologists, scientists, students and citizens can create their own history of water in their backyard, community, stream, spring or lake and join a global community of hydrology scientists and engineers who study and manage our water resources. Mobile Hydrology uses DBSCAN image processing to read water gauges planted in the reservoir and adds the information to the database of information collected from the same gauge.

ACL Risk Estimator is a web-application to provide means for Upper Peninsula (UP) high school (HS) coaches to get ACL injury risk assessment for their athletes and some education on prevention and mitigation of ACL injuries. Coaches or Athlete Trainers can upload videos of their athletes performing a jump, which later can be assessed by an injury expert to rate the ACL at risk on a scale of 1-10 along with feedback.
